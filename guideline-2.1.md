2- App's Purpose:
BigBlueBlocks is a simple puzzle game. The objective is to strategically place generated colored shapes into an 8x8 grid to complete horizontal and vertical lines, capturing sections and overcoming dynamically placed board hurdles. It offers engaging, brain training puzzle action intended for users of all ages looking for a casual yet challenging experience.

3- Instructions for Accessing and Reviewing:
The app is entirely self contained and functions locally without any form of authentication or internet connection. It does not require any account registration, meaning NO login credentials or dummy accounts are needed for Apple Review reviewers to explore the app. To review the app's core features:

    a- Launch the app, which directly seamlessly enters the main Game screen.
    b- Tap and hold unplaced colored shapes from the available piece pool at the bottom to preview their trace, then place them into valid spots on the central grid.
    c- Complete full vertical or horizontal lines to clear grid cells, increase score, and increment your level.
    d- As levels advance, observe the dynamic "hurdles" (blocked grid cells) being generated, adding increased difficulty.
    e- Access game settings (Sound, Vibration) by tapping the settings menu icon in the UI.
