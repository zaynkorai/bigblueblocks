Support

Need help with Big Blue Blocks? We're here for you.

1. Contact Information

If you are experiencing any bugs, crashes, or have other technical difficulties, please email us at: support@bigblueblocks.app.

We try our best to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.

2. Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I save my progress? Your progress is automatically saved to your device locally as you complete levels.
  • Do I need an internet connection to play? No, Big Blue Blocks is a completely offline experience.
  • Is my data safe? Yes. We do not collect analytics, and your settings and progress do not leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
