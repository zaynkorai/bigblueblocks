## 3. Graphic Asset Guidelines

To ensure your store listing converts clicks into installs, align your graphics with the dark, cybernetic aesthetic of the game.

### A. App Icon (512 x 512 px)
*   **Format**: 32-bit PNG (with alpha), maximum size 1 MB.
*   **Visual Direction**:
    *   Showcase a glowing blue 3D-like block shape or a grid of interlocking shapes.
    *   Set against the deep dark blue background (`#010816`).
    *   Keep the edges clean. **Do not add rounded corners or drop shadows**, as Google Play applies these automatically.
    *   Avoid text in the icon for maximum readability.

### B. Feature Graphic (1024 x 500 px)
*   **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha), maximum size 1 MB.
*   **Visual Direction**:
    *   Display the title "Big Blue Blocks" using the futuristic *Orbitron* font style.
    *   Position a colorful glowing grid in the center/right section, showcasing blocks landing and rows dissolving with electric sparks/starbursts.
    *   Avoid placing critical text elements or branding details near the borders (keep them within the central 60% safe zone) to ensure they aren't cropped on smaller devices.

### C. Phone & Tablet Screenshots (Min 3, Max 8)
*   **Format**: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio (e.g., 1080 x 1920 or 1920 x 1080 px).
*   **Recommended Screenshot Breakdown**:
    1.  **Home / Splash screen**: "Train Your Brain" – showing the dark futuristic interface and the "Tap to Play" prompt.
    2.  **Core Gameplay**: "Strategize & Fit Shapes" – demonstrating the 8x8 grid with blocks being dragged and line completion previews.
    3.  **Hurdles Feature**: "Conquer Dynamic Hurdles" – highlighting how blocked gray hurdles appear on the grid, increasing difficulty.
    4.  **Combos & Celebrations**: "Trigger Massive Combos" – showing lines bursting off the screen with starbursts and score popups.
    5.  **Offline Play**: "Play Anywhere, Offline" – indicating that no internet or login is required to play.
